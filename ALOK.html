EDUCATION

What Is Education?


Education is an important tool which is very useful in everybody's life.Education is what differentiate us from other living beings on earth.It empowers humans and get them ready to face challenges of life efficiently.

Importance Of Education:


Education is the most significant tool in eliminating poverty and unemployment.It enhances the self-confidence of a person and helps to take a better decision with the use of their knowledge.

Doorway To Success:


Education is a doorway to success which requires hard work and dedication.It serves as the key which will unlock numerous doors that will lead to success.
